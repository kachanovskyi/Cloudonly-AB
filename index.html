Сloudonly advantages

We provide you with all the tools you need to get started, we can help you with servers, databases, firewalls, storage solution, auto-scaling user management. Whatever you need, we can find a solution.

Reduce spending on technology infrastructure

Maintain easy access to your information with minimal upfront spending. Pay as you go (weekly, quarterly, or yearly), based on demand, which is a cost saver.

Reduce capital costs

Cloud eliminates the need for replacing capital expenditures on a regular basis, as well as avoiding spending on associated hardware, software or licensing fees.

Convenience savings

Since overheads are low, the services are available to use immediately helping to reduce revenue lost based on time suck associated with set-up.

Going green

Pooling resources into a cloud consolidates energy use. Businesses save on costs by paying for resources used.
